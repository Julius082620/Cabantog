Vision

The full extent of health problems caused by combining e-cigarettes with other tobacco products isn't yet clear. But dual users are more likely to report eye problems including dryness, redness, pain, blurry vision, light sensitivity, and headaches, according to a study of more than 4,000 adolescents and young adults.
